gamejam games

I'm a bit of a Touhou fanatic. Just a bit.

Because of that, I occasionally join game jams themed around Touhou. This resulted in the following:

  • Flee, Fairies, Flee! (2019), a janky little platformer where you control two characters at once through short but overly difficult stages. No, I haven't heard of the term "flow state", what are you talking about? Game design?
  • Youmu's Rampage (2020), an infinite scroller I made with the sole purpose to understand 2d skeletal animation a bit better. It's... better than the previous one? Possible? Mayhaps?
I like the phrase "jack of all trades"; all parts (graphics, audio, programming, etc) were made by just me in the 72h timeframe. Perhaps not the brightest of ideas.
